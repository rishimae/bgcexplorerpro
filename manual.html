User Manual

Dashboard

1. Saving Destinations

  • From the dashboard, choose a destination or location you want to save.
  • After selecting, click on the "Save" button.
  • Saved locations are accessible in the "Saved" section for future reference.

Saved Section

2. Managing Saved Locations

  • In the "Saved" section, view your list of saved locations.
  • To schedule a visit, click on the location and then click "Schedule."
  • Choose your preferred date and click "Confirm" to add it to your trips.

Trips Section

3. Managing Your Trips

  • Navigate to the "Trips" section to see your scheduled trips.
  • For each trip, you can:
    • Delete: Remove trips you no longer plan to take.
    • Explore: Click to view the location on a map for navigation.
    • Done: Mark a trip as completed after your visit.

Complete Section

4. Viewing Trip History

  • In the "Complete" section, review the history of all your completed trips within BGC.
  • This section provides a summary of where you've traveled and when.
